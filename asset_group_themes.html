High-Value Keyword Identification

Keywords like "muscle building protein powder", "protein powders for muscle gain", and "whey protein isolate" stand out due to their lower competition despite considerable search volume. Conversely, while "protein shakes" and "protein powder" have extremely high volume, their high competition and bid costs make them less efficient targets initially.

Pattern Recognition & Theme Ideation

The keywords reveal several key themes: muscle building and gain, weight loss, dietary restrictions (vegan, dairy-free), specific product types (whey isolate, casein), and brand-specific searches (MyProtein, Optimum Nutrition, Ghost). This suggests ad groups focused on fitness goals, dietary needs, product types, and brand loyalty.

Final Strategy & Grouping Logic

The final strategy will utilize these themes to create highly targeted ad groups. High-volume, low-competition keywords will anchor each theme, with supporting keywords adding breadth and specificity. Brand-specific keywords will be grouped separately to maximize conversion rates.
