High-Value Keyword Identification

Keywords like "chatgpt" (despite high competition), "chatgpt login in", and those containing "openai" show high search volume with low competition. The API-related keywords ("openai api", "openai api key") also present high-value opportunities, although with higher bid costs.

Pattern Recognition & Theme Ideation

The keywords fall into several categories: direct searches for ChatGPT and OpenAI, login and access-related queries, misspellings and variations of OpenAI, and broader AI-related searches. This suggests themes like "ChatGPT Access & Login", "OpenAI Products & Services", "General AI Information & Tools", and "AI-Specific Niches".

Final Strategy & Grouping Logic

The final strategy involves four main themes. "ChatGPT Access" focuses on direct searches and login issues. "OpenAI Ecosystem" groups keywords related to OpenAI's products and services. "Broad AI Exploration" targets general AI-related searches. "Advanced AI Applications" focuses on niche, high-value keywords like API access and specific AI tools.
